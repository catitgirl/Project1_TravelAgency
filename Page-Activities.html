What to do for those who does not want to take part in excursions


Spend your time on the beach!

Whichever island you choose, there are good beaches everywhere in Greece. By the number of the cleanest beaches, Greece takes one of the first places in Europe. More than 14 thousand kilometers of the Greek coast can guarantee an excellent beach holiday for every taste

Give attention to shopping

In Greece, if you believe the old proverb, you can find everything. Despite the fact that Greece is not one of the most popular shopping destinations in Europe, you can make really good deals here. Don't forget to buy some souvenirs for friends and family too

Aquaparks

If you love water activities and slides, then Greece is the right choice! Water parks here fit every taste, no one will be disappointed! In addition, one of the largest water parks is located here: on Crete, be sure to go to "Aqua Splash", on Rhodes - to "Water Park" in the Faliraki area

Fishing

Not everyone knows that fishing in Greece is a national sport that everyone practices with great pleasure.If you dream of fishing like a local and would like to meet the people who live here and are known for their hospitality and fishing traditions - then you should urgently go to Greece

Spearfishing

Spearfishing with a harpoon or a spear is very popular on the scoast of the Greek islands. During spearfishing, use diving buoys, since there is heavy traffic around the islands of jet skis and small yachts, which, in turn, must maintain a distance of 100 m from diving buoys

Wants to feel extreme?

For fans of active sports, Greece is ready to provide unlimited possibilities. On numerous beaches of the country you can go windsurfing and diving, ride a yacht, including beaches that are not accessible by land. Followers of extreme sports will also be able to do rafting on small, but very stormy mountain rivers

Go walking

Take a walk in nature. The magnificent landscapes of Greece are amazing, the warm climate makes hiking possible throughout the year

Night life

When evaluating Greece as a potential destination for your vacation, you should not think that Hellas has only beaches and archaeological sites. The Greek islands are famous for partying all night long
